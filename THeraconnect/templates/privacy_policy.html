Privacy Policy

Introduction

TheraConnect respects your privacy and is committed to protecting your personal information. This Privacy Policy explains what information we collect, how we use it, and the steps we take to ensure it is handled responsibly.

1. Information We Collect

  • Personal Information: Includes your name, contact information, health-related information, and any other information provided during registration or in communications with our platform.
  • Usage Data: Includes data on how you use the app, including session duration, accessed resources, and features used.
  • Sensitive Information: We may collect health information only with your explicit consent, as required to connect you with licensed therapists.

2. How We Use Your Information

  • To provide and improve our services, match you with therapists, and manage appointments.
  • To comply with legal obligations or as otherwise permitted by law.
  • For research or analytics purposes, where data is anonymized and aggregated.

3. Sharing Your Information

  • With Therapists: Shared only to facilitate services and with your consent.
  • Third Parties: We do not sell your information. We may share anonymized data with third parties for research or operational improvements.

4. Security Measures

We use secure servers, encrypted connections, and strict access control to protect your information. However, no method of transmission over the internet or electronic storage is 100% secure.

5. Your Rights

You have the right to access or correct your information, delete or restrict its use, and withdraw consent where applicable.

6. Updates to This Policy

We may update this policy periodically. The latest version will always be accessible on our website.
